Monday Anime Night

Join us every Monday at 7 p.m. for our weekly anime watching session. Gurren Lagaan is our set series so we will watch two episodes of that per week, before watching three episodes of other anime decided by a vote at the start.

Hop on our Discord to join in.

Manga Book Club

We have a manga book club meeting every two weeks. We read the first volume of a manga and then meet up on the Discord to discuss it and rate the manga.

The next session is reading "Can you become a magical girl even at XX?" and we will be meeting at 7 p.m. on Wednesday the 28th of October.

Pumpkin Carving Competition

Finally, we have our Halloween event, a Pumpkin Carving Competition. This is a competition wherein you should send in pictures of your completed Jack'o'Lanterns before midnight of the 30th of October, after which the committee will be judging them. The winner will be getting a £15 Amazon voucher, with smaller vouchers for the runners-up. Bonus points will be awarded for particularly anime inspired pumpkins. Either put your pictures on the Discord and ping @committee, or email them to us at committee@nottsanime.co.uk
