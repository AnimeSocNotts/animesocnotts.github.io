Monday Anime Night

Join us every Monday at 7 p.m. for our weekly anime watching session. Gurren Lagaan is our set series so we will watch two episodes of that per week, before watching three episodes of other anime decided by a vote at the start.

Hop on our Discord to join in.

Manga Book Club

We have a manga book club meeting every two weeks. We read the first volume of a manga and then meet up on the Discord to discuss it and rate the manga.

The next session is reading Way of the House Husband and we will be meeting at 7 p.m. on Wednesday the 11th of November.

Movie Night

We have a movie screening night on the Wednesdays without the Manga Book Club at 7 p.m. Join us in watching anime movies of all shapes and sizes.

The next session will be at 7 p.m. on the 4th of November.
