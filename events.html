London Comic Con Trip

On Saturday the 23th of October we will be going to London Comic Con. Make sure to get your Comic Con tickets and head on over to the SU website to purchase a ticket (£10) on the AnimeSoc coach to get you there. You need to be signed into the SU site before you can purchase the ticket. After you have brought your ticket you need to fill in this short form.

Anime Watch Night

When: 7pm, Monday          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

Our bread and butter, our anime watching night!

We have a set series, Samurai Champloo, of which we will watch two episodes of per week, before watching three episodes of other anime decided by a vote at the start.

Manga Book Club

When: 7pm, Wednesday          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

We have a manga book club meeting every two weeks. We read the first volume of a manga and then meet up on the Discord to discuss it and rate the manga. For the next session, on Wednesday the 3rd of November we will be reading Kuro.

Wednesdays are also used for AnimeSoc movie screenings, which will cover a variety of genres of anime movies. See below for details.

Halloween Pumpkin Carving

When: 7pm, Saturday 30th October          Where: LG11, Trent Building

A longtime society tradition. Come along to carve a pumpkin! Afterward we will play some fun party games. Pumpkins are provided.
