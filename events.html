Committee Choice Night

On Wednesday the 10th of February at 7pm we have a committee choice night, where the members of the committee will be bringing along some of their favourites for everyone to watch.

Monday Anime Night

Join us every Monday at 7 pm for our weekly anime watching session. We have a set series, currently the Great Pretender, of which we will watch two episodes of per week, before watching three episodes of other anime decided by a vote at the start.

Hop on our Discord to join in.

Manga Book Club

We have a manga book club meeting every two weeks. We read the first volume of a manga and then meet up on the Discord to discuss it and rate the manga.

The next session is discussing Kusuriya no Hitorigoto, a historical drama about a young pharmacist working as a servant in the imperial court of ancient China. We will be meeting on Wednesday the 17th of February at 7 pm, and will be discussing the first 8 chapters.

Anime Lightning Talks

Saturday 20th at 7pm will be the Anime Lightning Talks event. Come along and present one or two short talks about something anime related you really like. It'll be a great chance to learn something new about anime and to get to know our fellow members' passions and interests.
