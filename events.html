Our bread and butter, our anime watching night!

Manga Book Club

When: 7pm, Wednesday          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

We have a manga book club meeting every two weeks. We read the first volume of a manga and then meet up on the Discord to discuss it and rate the manga. For the next session, on Wednesday the 1st of December we will be reading first volume (7 chapters) of Sousou no Frieren!

Wednesdays are also used for AnimeSoc movie screenings, which will cover a variety of genres of anime movies. See below for details.

No Game, No Life: Zero Screening

When: 7pm, Wednesday 8th December          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

No Game, No Life: Zero is a story about humanity in a world of conflict between incomprehensibly powerful beings, and one fated meeting between a young warrior looking to ensure humanities survival and a machine who wants to understand the human heart. Follow this unlikely pair as they attempt to understand what it is to be human, and work to bring an end to the eternal war.

It is a prequel movie to the much acclaimed series No Game, No Life, however there is no requirement that you have watched the main series, as it is an entirely separate story.
