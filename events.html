Anime Watch Night

When: 7pm, Monday          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

Our bread and butter, our anime watching night!

We have a set series, Bakemonogatari, of which we will watch two episodes of per week, before watching three episodes of other anime decided by a vote at the start.

Manga Book Club

When: 7pm, Wednesday          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

We have a manga book club meeting every two weeks. We read the first volume of a manga and then meet up to discuss it and rate the manga. For the next session, on Wednesday the 23rd of February we will be reading first volume (8 chapters) of Lucifer and the Biscuit Hammer!

Wednesdays are also used for AnimeSoc movie screenings, which will cover a variety of genres of anime movies. See below for details.
