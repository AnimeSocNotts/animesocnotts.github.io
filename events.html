Anime Watch Night

When: 7pm, Monday          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

Our bread and butter, our anime watching night!

We have a set series, Samurai Champloo, of which we will watch two episodes of per week, before watching three episodes of other anime decided by a vote at the start.

Manga Book Club

When: 7pm, Wednesday          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

We have a manga book club meeting every two weeks. We read the first volume of a manga and then meet up on the Discord to discuss it and rate the manga. For the next session, on Wednesday the 20th of October we will be readingchapters 1 to 7 of Yokohama Kaidashi Kikou.

Wednesdays are also used for AnimeSoc movie screenings, which will cover a variety of genres of anime movies. See below for details.

Clamp Night

For our first theme night we will be watching a selection of anime based upon the works of legendary manga group CLAMP.
