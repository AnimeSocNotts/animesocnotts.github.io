Binge Afternoon - Flip Flappers

When: 2pm, Saturday 20th November          Where: A46, Trent Building

Prove your anime endurance by coming along and watching an entire series in one go! 

Flip Flappers is the adventure of two girls with clashing personalities, the serious Cocona, and the energetic Papika, who travel into the dreamlike alternate worlds of "Pure Illusion" to find crystal shards for the mysterious organization Flip Flap, all the while trying to sort out their feelings about each other. Who are Flip Flap, and what do they want with the crystal shards? Will Cocona and Papika come to understand each other? Find out by coming to watch!

The show is 13 episodes long, we'll be starting at 2pm, and will probably finish around 8pm, giving time for a break in the middle.

No Game, No Life: Zero Screening

When: 7pm, Wednesday 8th December          Where: A41, Clive Granger Building

No Game, No Life: Zero is a story about humanity in a world of conflict between incomprehensibly powerful beings, and one fated meeting between a young warrior looking to ensure humanities survival and a machine who wants to understand the human heart. Follow this unlikely pair as they attempt to understand what it is to be human, and work to bring an end to the eternal war.

It is a prequel movie to the much acclaimed series No Game, No Life, however there is no requirement that you have watched the main series, as it is an entirely separate story.
